(02) Why is Another Hendrick's so alluring?

ANOTHER HENDRICK’S is what happens when a spirit discovers another side of itself. You might think of Another Hendrick’s as a twin, yet not an identical one. Although it shares the same rounded balance and peculiar soul as Hendrick’s original, it expresses that soul in a different sensory dimension, thanks to its unpredictable infusions of Orange Blossom & Cacao. Although Ms. Lesley Gracie avoids “edges and points” in the tastes of her creations, she is entirely comfortable with frontiers and horizons. To our knowledge, we are the first gin maker to discover another side of ourselves - and commit to distilling it. But then again, we have always suspected there was more than one way to be Hendrick’s.
