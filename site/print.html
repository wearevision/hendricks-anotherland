Act I

The Welcome

In the entrance, the Herbarium stands before the door — silent, watchful.

Guests present their key. The door opens. The ordinary world is left behind.

Inside, the Storytellers welcome guests into Hendrick's Origins — gathering around the real copper alembic for the first act of the night.

Welcome drink: Hendrick's Original with cucumber and rose.

The Botanical
Sorbets

Between the choices and the revelation — two artisanal sorbets that bridge both worlds.

Cucumber & Bulgarian Rose — inspired by the Original's perfect serve. Cool, green, herbal. Delicate and fresh.

Orange Blossom & Cacao — inspired by Another's signature infusion. Warm, amber, dark chocolate depth. Unmistakably Another.

Act III

The WOW Moment

The figure rises. An invitation. All the lights turn on — and Anotherland is revealed in its full, breathtaking glory.

Act III — The Toast

The Discovery

The Orator reveals the wooden box — it opens to unveil Hendrick's Another, a gin uniquely infused with orange blossom and cacao.

Some lights shift to orange. The cocktail built through the story is served — and the toast is raised.

"To Another,
may we savor it always."
Phase 3 — Amplification

The Unbox

Experienced by few. Seen by many.

The 60+ curious souls who received the mysterious invitation but were not among the chosen 40 find a seeding box at their door within days of the event.

They unbox it on camera. They share it with their followers. The hashtag #hendrickschile spreads. The legend of Anotherland grows.
